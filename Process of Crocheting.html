Process of Learning Crochet

\                                                                                                                      

                                                                             First is to know how to hold a hook which can either be Pencil grip or Knife grip. Remember that it's important to learn how to hold

                                                               your hook because of tension. Tension is important in crochet because it affects the overall appearance and size of your project.


                                                                             There are a lot of stitches that we can learn in order to progress this include the two basic knots called Slip knot and Chain Stitch.

                                                               After learning the two basics knot we can now move to the stitches called Single Crochet (SC), Double Crochet (DC), and finally the finishing.
